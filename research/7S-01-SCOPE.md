# 7S-01: SCOPE
**Library**: simple_social
**Status**: BACKWASH (retroactive documentation)
**Date**: 2026-01-23

## What Problem Does This Solve?

simple_social monitors social media platforms for engagement opportunities:
- Discover relevant conversations on Reddit, Stack Overflow, Hacker News, etc.
- Score opportunities by relevance
- Generate and manage response drafts
- Track engagements and replies
- Store curated resources for reference

## Target Users

1. **Developer Advocates** - Monitoring for technology discussions
2. **Content Marketers** - Finding engagement opportunities
3. **Community Managers** - Tracking brand mentions

## Domain

Social media monitoring and engagement tracking.

## In-Scope

- Multi-platform scanning (Reddit, Stack Overflow, HN, GitHub, Dev.to)
- Keyword-based opportunity discovery
- Relevance scoring
- Draft management with AI generation support
- Engagement tracking
- Reply monitoring
- Resource curation database
- SQLite persistence

## Out-of-Scope

- Automated posting (human review required)
- Real-time streaming
- Sentiment analysis
- Brand monitoring
- Paid advertising management
